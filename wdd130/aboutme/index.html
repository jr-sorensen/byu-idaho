About Me

Jessica Sorensen

Hello everyone! My name is Jessica Sorensen. I live in Southern, CA, but not the cool beachy part. I live in the high-desert, close to Death Valley. Sadly I hate the desert, SO I try to escape the ugly brown, by visiting my in-laws up in beautifully green Tacoma, WA.

My major is in Applied Technology as I am working on my BS in Science. I am currently working my way through front end development. I have really enjoyed everything I have learned so far. I am hoping that I will be able to find a career after graduating that will allow me to work remotely.

I am a stay at home, married mother of 5 kiddos, so I am always busy carting kids around. I also breed AKC Golden Retrievers and Goldendoodles. Raising puppies is my favorite thing to do in my limited spare time. I also love to read and to spend time outside when it's not too hot (it was 115 degrees last week). I just added 16 hen chicks to our crazy little homestead, so I am figuring all that out as well. Any tips would be greatly appreciated!
